The bar

Small bar.
Big bagels.

Kairos Coffee Bar is a small takeaway spot tucked into Block 2 of Northgate Park, Brooklyn. We're built for speed and quality over square footage — most of what we make is in your hands within minutes of ordering.

We keep two tables outside for anyone who wants to sit a while, but the real point of Kairos is timing: getting the right coffee to the right person at exactly the moment they need it. That, and bagels we'd put up against anyone in Cape Town.

We also bring the bar to you with Kairos Coffee Trailer, a pop-up coffee trailer for events, markets, office mornings, and private gatherings.
